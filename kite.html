Kite

A better way to stay in touch

The skinny

Overview

Duration / 3 weeks

Tools / Figma, Zoom, Maze

Roles / UX Research, Content Strategy, Information Architecture, Visual Design

Deliveries / Research Plan, Competitive Analysis, User Survey, User Interviews, Personas, User Journey, User Stories, User Flow, Sitemap, Wireframes, Branding, Usability Testing, High-Fidelity Prototype

The brief

Problem

Providers of telecommunications service to prisons have secured contracts worth $1.6b but have not yet developed seamless design solutions that enhance the communication experience for incarcerated individuals and their loved ones.

The MVP

Outcome

My aim was to design a solution that would improve upon the user experience of inmate communication apps by modernizing the design and collaborating with friends and family of the incarcerated to ultimately deliver a solution with true user value.
